SUMMARY


I devised an eye-tracker setup and developed the helper module for pupil-labs products in Python (published on PyPI and Github). as a part of near-eye display project. To reduce the form factor, we tried to eliminate World Camera of Eye tracker, thus had to implement our own calibration function. To calibrate eye-tracker data, we collect some data before measuring based on target prepared and process it by using K-mean clustering and affine transform. The results are shown on the right. You can check out more functions that eases your data processing on pypupil-docs.
