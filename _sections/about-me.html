Hi, I am a first-year PhD student at Stanford University in Electrical Engineering, working as a part of Stanford Computational Imaging Lab, advised by Prof. Gordon Wetztsein. I am interested in developing 3D display hardware systems with novel algorithmic frameworks. Generally my research interests include computational displays, optimization techniques for optical systems, and light transport. I am supported by a Kwanjeong Scholarship and a Korea Government Scholarship.

Previously, I received my Bachelor's degree in ECE at Seoul National University, where I was fortunate to be a recipient of The Presidential Science Scholarship and be advised by Prof. Deog-Kyoon Jeong, Prof. Soo-Mook Moon, and Prof. Byoungho Lee. Before that, I was a silver medalist at the IPhO (International Physics Olympiad) 2012.
