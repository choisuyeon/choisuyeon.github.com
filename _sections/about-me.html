Short bio
I am an Assistant Professor of Computer Science and Engineering at Seoul National University. My research focuses on developing novel algorithms and optical systems at the intersection of graphics, optics, artificial intelligence, and vision science. During my PhD, I developed holographic display systems incorporating machine learning towards next-generation VR/AR displays, and my thesis received the ACM SIGGRAPH Outstanding Dissertation Award Honorable Mention in 2025. My research has been supported by a Meta Research PhD Fellowship, a SPIE Optics and Photonics Education Scholarship, a Kwanjeong Scholarship, a Korean Government Scholarship, and a GPU gift from NVIDIA. I did my PhD at Stanford University with Prof. Gordon Wetzstein and undergrad at Seoul National University.
